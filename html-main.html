Visualizing Etymology

Exploring the origins of words

This graph shows etymologically related words to the chosen center word. The center word has a fill color black, all words that originate from this,have darker links/nodes and all words that are origins of this word have lighter links/nodes. To trace a path from a node to the center node, hover on the word/node. Blue lines indicate origin nodes and black lines indicate child nodes. You can click on any node view it's tree, or search for a word in the search box. The depth of this search is limited to 5 links. You can also add a new word in to see if there is a tree from the new node to our current center node. If such a path is present, a tree will be drawn with the new word in center branching out to the current center node (on the edges now). This depth is also limited to 5 steps (from each way: so in total 10 steps).
