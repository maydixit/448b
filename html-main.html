Visualizing Etymology

Exploring the origins of words

This graph shows etymologically related words to the chosen center word. The center word has a fill color black, all words that originate from this, have darker links/nodes and all words that are origins of this word have lighter links/nodes. To trace a path from a node to the center node, use hover. Blue lines indicate origin nodes and black lines indicate child nodes. You can click on any node view it's tree, or search for a word in the search box.
